Pint Pints is a small business that has built an app that would allow users to navigate to find either the closest bar near them or would like to know if a specific location that has a bars in that surrounded area. They decided to take on this project to help the users find the nearest bar where they can take their friends or for they own curiosity.

Users can view the web application/app as a guest or you can be a registered user. The guests will be able to see information about the bars and there ratings. However, they will not be able to rate the bar or provide feedback. While registered users can rate and comment on each of the bars they have been to and use it as a tool to track which ones they have visited.

Once you have registered, you will get a 90 day trial of using the app and all its features but once your trial has ended you would need to pay of the app for the up keeping. It will cost users to become a member £5.99 and this will last yearly. This application is an ongoing upgrade & maintenance

If users exploit or caused problems using this application the person will be dealt with in a fair manner. either a warning or banned.
